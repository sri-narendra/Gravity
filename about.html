About Gravity

We are not just a student organization. We are a family with enthusiastic members who stand together for a common cause.

Who We Are

GRAVITY is a 3-tier Organization started on 10th OCT 2019 at Lovely Professional University. Since the beginning we have grown exponentially and this was stated by our mentors. Our creative family has already marked a unique name in the university.

The primary idea of Gravity is to create a platform which makes people realize that everyone is good at something and make them feel confident about themselves by making them participate in their respected interested genres.

"After all, confidence is the key."

Our Story

Like Newton, an apple made it happen. The unfulfilled dream of every college student is to eat healthy, and to quit junk food. In this process, one of our founding members was having an apple around 5-6 in the evening and met his old friend having an extraordinary talent of watching web-series.

His friend then quoted: "What if watching web series would acquire me a certificate, after all it needs a lot of patience and I can add this in my CV."

That moment we realized we should create some unorthodox events that people would truly enjoy.

Our Focus Areas

Public Speaking

Build confidence as a speaker by learning how to use simple tools and skills to prepare and deliver memorable presentations.

Content Writing

Presenting an idea in a clear and easy medium so that a large number of people could connect to it.

Photography

Capturing a memory in a way that most of us get nostalgic the moment we see it.

Event Management

Organizing the event in a way we planned and also to manage something that goes in an unplanned way.

Our Core Values

Trust

People should trust us for investing their time and efforts with us.

Participation

Engagement of all the members is important.

Satisfaction

After sparing time for us, they should be positively satisfied.
